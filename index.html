Adding Color: The RGB Channels

AI simplifies millions of colors into three "sheets": **Red, Green, and Blue**. A color photo is just three spreadsheets stacked together!

The Formula:

Pixel(x,y) = [R] + [G] + [B]

If the Red sheet says 255 and others say 0, the AI sees pure Red. If all sheets have high numbers, it sees bright, vibrant colors or White!
